Goku

Goku is a fictional character who is the main character in Dragon Ball Z. Dragon Ball Z is about Goku went through many struggles in his quest to protect the world. He had to kill his own brother to protect the world. Goku is not actually human but a alien from a planet called Vegeta.

Goku is a Saiyan, the strongest warriors in whole entire universe. Every time they fight they get stronger and eventually reach a point where they became a Super Saiyan. Goku became the first Super Saiyan. During his journey, Goku died but was resurrected by his friends through Dragon Balls. Goku worked hard to protect the world. Goku was able to make many friends and trustworthy companions that would fight alongside him in his quest to protect the world.
